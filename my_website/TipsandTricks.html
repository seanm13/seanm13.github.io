Tips and Tricks


Tip 1: The best way to learn coding is by practicing. You should practice different techniques as well as styles.

Tip 2: Debugging can take a long time, so to work in the most efficient way, take breaks every now and then.

Tip 3: There are many online resources that can help when you aren't sure how to do something. One example is www.w3schools.com.
